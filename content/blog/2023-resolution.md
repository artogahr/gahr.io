2022 was an excellent year. I got married, moved to Prague, made good friends, and went to a Men I Trust concert. But there's room to improve, and I'm going into 2023 with some actual goals.

## Health

Everything is melting. Accumulated stress from work, marriage paperwork, military service obligations, and relocating has taken a toll. Health is my primary focus this year.

### Physical

Weight is the main concern. I'm currently around 99.8 kg and my ADHD makes it hard to maintain habits once momentum breaks. I'm not going to try a restrictive diet—intermittent fasting feels more sustainable.

The plan for January is a gradual shift: move my eating window progressively later until I reach a 16:8 schedule by the end of the month. Goal: lose 2-3 kg to reach 97 kg.

### Mental

Stress is making me irritable, unmotivated, and scattered. I'd like to try therapy, but finding English-speaking practitioners covered by Czech insurance is genuinely difficult and expensive. My wife has suggested meditation. I remain resistant, but I haven't ruled it out.

## Work

### Rust

I got interested in Rust this year. I like the combination of performance, safety, and expressiveness. I've read The Book, watched videos, and worked through some Advent of Code challenges. I want to go further.

Currently I'm a TAC Engineer at Fortinet—essentially tech support. The role is stable with decent work-life balance, but I'm not excited by it. I'm looking for a software development position, preferably something involving Rust.

### Projects

Things I want to build:

- **Artodo** — a personal todo app (already started)
- **Text insertion tool** — customizable clipboard for frequently-used phrases at work
- **Personal website** — possibly a Rust-based replacement for my tilde.team presence
- **Torrent server automation** — Sonarr + Lidarr for my existing media setup

I also finally created a Mastodon account at [tilde.zone/@artogahr](https://tilde.zone/@artogahr) and deleted Twitter.
